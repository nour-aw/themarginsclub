About The Margins Club

Born from quiet readings at Midnight Run Café in Waterloo, The Margins Club is not a traditional book club. We’re a small, monthly collective for reading, creative reflection, and conversation, a home for the notes and ideas that fall in the margins. Bring a book, a journal, or yourself; we’ll supply the table and company.

Once upon a time in Waterloo, a small group gathered at Midnight Run Café to read side by side, no assigned books, no agenda, simply presence. From those evenings something unexpected grew: strangers became friends, and conversation grew out of margins and sticky notes.

Our values:
  • Intimacy over scale.
  • Curiosity over expertise.
  • Generous listening.

Our evenings include short readings, open conversation, and a little time at the end for people to annotate, literally, with sticky notes, pens, and conversation starters.

Want to participate? Check our events page or drop a note in the reading log.
