Read beyond the margins

Since 2012, The Margins Club has helped readers discover voices that stretch perspective and spark imagination. We pair carefully curated reading lists with intimate gatherings and creative experiences so members can connect ideas to lived experience.

Curated collections

Quarterly reading journeys spotlight independent presses, translations, and debut voices—accompanied by thoughtful guides and conversation prompts.

Living room salons

Gather with fellow members in salons hosted by local partners, featuring guest authors, artists, and scholars who bring the books to life.

Field notes

Monthly dispatches from contributors around the world capture the intersections of literature, culture, and community.

Membership pathways

Choose the rhythm that fits your reading life. Every membership includes access to our digital archive, community forums, and live events with sliding-scale pricing to keep the club accessible.

Companion

A gentle pace with quarterly books, private forums, and optional salon access. Ideal for new members finding their reading rhythm.

Salon Circle

Monthly book boxes, live author salons, and themed discussion pods curated by our facilitators.

Founders Table

An immersive experience with seasonal retreats, editorial collaborations, and opportunities to shape future programming.
